# Safety And Use Policy

GOSP Forge foundation examples are educational screening artifacts. They are not potable-water certification, professional engineering approval, production manufacturing approval, or real-world deployment approval.

## Validation Rules

- Physical, water, and electrical modules require a safety profile.
- Water and electrical module safety profiles must state no potable-water claim and no professional-use claim.
- Content that claims potable-water certification, professional approval, or production readiness is blocked by validation.
- Education profiles are instructional context only. They produce warnings and do not imply safety approval.

## Clean Water Boundary

The Clean Water examples model classroom tradeoffs for flow, power, cost, and confidence. They do not certify treated water as safe to drink and must remain supervised classroom demonstrations unless independently reviewed outside this repository.
