# Clean Water Student Guide

This guide is free for public-school use.

## Safety

Work only with supervised classroom materials. This activity has no potable-water claim, does not prove water is safe to drink, and is not professional engineering approval. Do not drink, taste, or distribute any water from a classroom demonstration.

## Investigation

Use the project manifest, modules, products, simulation output, estimate output, and validation warnings to answer:

- What parts of the system move water?
- What parts use power?
- Which inputs are assumptions or defaults?
- How do warnings change confidence?
- What questions would need expert review before real-world use?

## Access

No account, purchase, donation, fee, or subscription is required from students for this foundation classroom activity.
