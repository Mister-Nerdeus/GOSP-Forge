# Simulation Confidence Summary

Simulation envelopes include a confidenceSummary that counts known, defaulted, and unknown inputs plus warning count and graphWarningCount.

Low confidence is visible when defaulted or unknown inputs remain, when simulation warnings are present, or when Clean Water graph consistency warnings are present.

Graph consistency checks only compare declared graph node coverage against required foundation Clean Water concepts. They do not execute arbitrary graph logic and do not imply professional validation.

The clean-water model remains a Level-1 educational screening model and does not certify potable water or professional use.
