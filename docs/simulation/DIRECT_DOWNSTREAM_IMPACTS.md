# Direct And Downstream Impacts

Clean Water simulation output separates direct impacts from downstream impacts.

Direct impacts describe immediate modeled outputs, such as the level-1 modeled pump flow result. Downstream impacts describe dependent effects such as power/runtime compatibility and cost confidence implications.

Downstream impacts are educational indicators only. Unknown or simplified downstream effects must remain visible as warnings or limitations. They are not potable-water certification, professional engineering review, production manufacturing approval, or field validation.
