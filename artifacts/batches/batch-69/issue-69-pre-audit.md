# Issue 69 Pre-Audit

## Scope

Add direct/downstream impact reporting to Clean Water simulation output.

## Current Behavior

- Simulation output included flow, power, and envelope data.
- Direct and downstream impacts were not separated.
- Downstream uncertainty was not named as impact-report warning/limitation.

## Risk Notes

Impact reports must remain educational and must not imply professional, potable-water, production, or field validation.
