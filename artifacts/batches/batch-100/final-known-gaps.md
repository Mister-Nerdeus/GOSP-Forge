# Final Known Gaps

Date: 2026-05-14

These gaps are not hidden and must remain non-claims until future issues implement, test, review, and close them.

- UI is read-only inspection only; no editing, persistence, CAD, routing, auth, deployment, or production workflow exists.
- Browser automation is postponed until UI workflows expand beyond inspection.
- Clean Water simulation is level-1 educational screening only, not professional validation or field validation.
- Estimate confidence remains low because product quantities and some lifecycle inputs are defaulted or missing.
- Fabrication and price assumptions are classroom/conceptual assumptions, not market pricing or production manufacturing approval.
- API behavior is local foundation behavior only; there is no production hosting, identity, authorization, managed secret storage, or production persistence.
- Manufacturer verification is not implemented; draft import records cannot mark AI-extracted data as manufacturer-verified.
- Registry/import checks are foundation governance checks and not legal advice.
- No public leaderboard, public marketplace, production storage, production deployment readiness, or production security certification exists.
- Historical batch audits may contain issue-time statements; current status is represented by the final closeout and final audit docs.
